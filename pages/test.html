    Mission:
    Every child has the right to quality education but due to in lack of resources in rural villages this is very difficult for them.We are in the mission of providing them quality education and skill development education.


    Admission guidelines



    To ensure admission in pre-primary classes and class 1st the age criterion is as follows

       Class Age as on 1st April

    Nursery 2 years 8 months
    KG 3 years 8 months
    Prep. 4 years 8 months
    1st 5 years 8 months



    Academics

    Pre-Primary

    Attested photocopy of municipal birth certificate.


    Class 1 onwards


    School leaving certificate from school last attended.

    Re-Admission


    Regular absence without leave or unexplained absence for more than 20 consecutive working days renders the student liable to his/her name being struck off from the school rolls.



    General Rules


    The amount deposited towards registration and admission for a Particular year will neither be refunded nor transferred to any other year nor to any other child.
